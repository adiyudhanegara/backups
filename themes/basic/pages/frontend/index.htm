{# About Us #}

About Us

Genta Sari Silver welcomes you to discover the artistry of Balinese silver jewelry in Celuk, Bali. Situated in the heart of this historic village renowned for its silver craftsmanship, we are dedicated to preserving and innovating upon centuries-old techniques. Our passion lies in creating exquisite jewelry that not only enhances beauty but also celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Bali.

Expertise

Drawing from the expertise of skilled artisans, Genta Sari Silver specializes in crafting a wide range of silver jewelry pieces. From intricate filigree work to contemporary designs, our artisans use high-quality sterling silver combined with locally sourced gemstones to create unique earrings, necklaces, rings, and bracelets. Each piece showcases meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail, ensuring that every customer receives a timeless piece of art.

Experiences

With decades of experience in the industry, Genta Sari Silver has earned a reputation for excellence and authenticity. Our journey began with a commitment to uphold traditional silver techniques while adapting to modern tastes and trends. We take pride in serving a global clientele, providing personalized service and unique designs that resonate with our customers' preferences and aspirations.

Basic Silver Crafting Class
Learn the fundamentals of silver crafting under the guidance of our skilled artisans. This class is perfect for beginners looking to understand basic techniques like soldering, shaping, and polishing.

Intermediate Silver Jewelry Design
Dive deeper into the art of silver jewelry design. This intermediate class covers advanced techniques such as stone setting, filigree work, and intricate soldering.

Advanced Artisan Workshop
Master the artistry of silver jewelry making in our advanced workshop. This class is designed for experienced craftsmen interested in refining their skills with complex designs, custom orders, and professional finishing techniques.
